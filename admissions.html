Frequently Asked Questions

What age can my child start school?

Children may begin from Crèche level according to the school's admission policy.


Do you admit pupils during the term?

Yes. Mid-term admissions may be considered subject to available space.


Is transportation available?

Yes. Transportation services are available on selected routes.


Do you provide feeding services?

Yes. Nutritious meals are available for pupils enrolled in the feeding programme.
